What is LACC?

LACC is a series of small coding challanges that are very losely defined. The rules are simple, make something, then share it. The focus is on creating something and being creative, and that doesn't allways translate to a "pleasing" end result.

Think of it like creative writing prompts, but for programming. How it is made, doesn't matter. How it's working, doesn't matter. The theme is revealed areand 17:00 Swedish time, and just like in kindergarten everyone is a winner.

To join, just post your output in the LiTHe kod "LACC" slack-channel . And just like LiU's exams, everything is allowed, except stealing other peoples work.
